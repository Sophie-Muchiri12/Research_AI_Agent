Baking a Cake Step by Step

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).

Step 2: Prepare the ingredients: flour, sugar, eggs, butter, milk, and vanilla extract.

Step 3: Mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar) in a bowl.

Step 4: Mix the wet ingredients (eggs, butter, milk, vanilla extract) in another bowl.

Step 5: Combine the dry and wet ingredients and mix until smooth.

Step 6: Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.

Step 7: Bake the cake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Step 8: Let the cake cool before serving.
